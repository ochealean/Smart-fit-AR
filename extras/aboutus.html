About SmartFit

Transforming the footwear retail experience through innovation and technology

Our Story

SmartFit is an innovative platform designed to transform the footwear retail experience through the integration of augmented reality and AI-powered assistance. It was developed to address the challenges faced by both customers and retailers, offering a smarter and more convenient way to explore, customize, and purchase footwear.

Through features such as augmented reality try-ons, customization tools, and intelligent recommendations, SmartFit empowers customers to make confident purchasing decisions while supporting retailers in reaching a wider audience and enhancing their services.

How SmartFit Works

User Registration & Account Management

The proposed system will allow all users to register on the platform by filling out a sign-up form to create an account. It will allow the shop owners and employees to efficiently manage shoe inventory, process orders, and update product availability. With the use of the proposed system, authorized employees can track transactions, monitor stock levels, and oversee customization requests, ensuring a streamlined and efficient retail experience.

Augmented Reality Experience

Through the use of augmented reality integration, registered customers can virtually interact and visualize shoes before making a purchase that allows them to explore different styles, colors, and designs in real time.

Ordering System

Through the use of the ordering system, customers can easily place shoe orders through a user-friendly interface that streamlines the purchasing process. Shop owners and employees can manage orders, update product availability, and process transactions.

Customization Features

Through the use of the customization feature, customers can personalize their shoe selections by choosing designs, colors, and materials while receiving accurate cost estimates. Shop owners and employees can review customization requests, confirm feasibility, and process personalized orders efficiently.

Order Tracking

Through the use of the order tracking system, the customers can monitor the status of their purchases in real time. Shop owners and employees can update order statuses to keep customers informed about the progress of their purchases.

AI-Powered Support & Feedback

Through the integration of AI-driven customer support, customers receive timely and accurate assistance for their inquiries. Also, the feedback system will allow the customers to submit ratings and reviews, providing valuable insights into product quality and service satisfaction.

Reporting & Analytics

The system features report generation, enabling shop owners and employees to collect and organize data for efficient analysis of sales, inventory, and customer feedback.
