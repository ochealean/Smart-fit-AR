Shoe Parts Information

Sole: The bottom part of the shoe that provides cushioning and traction.

Upper: The part of the shoe that covers the top of the foot.

Laces: Used to secure the shoe to the foot.

Tongue: The flap under the laces that protects the top of the foot.

Heel: The back part of the shoe that supports the heel.

Toe Box: The front part that protects the toes.
